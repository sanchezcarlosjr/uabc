### Tuts+ Tutorial: From the Top: Adobe Photoshop for Beginners

#### Instructor: Daniel Scott

In this hands on course, we will work through examples of typical creative challenges you will encounter as you learn to master Photoshop. We start at the absolute beginning and work quickly into more really exciting techniques. We will learn adjusting colors, masking, retouching & manipulating images. Whether you are a photographer, marketer or designer, this is the perfect class to gain core Photoshop skills.
